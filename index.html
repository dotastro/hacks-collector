.Astronomy Hacks Collector

This site is a collection of Hack Day projects from the .Astronomy conference series. If you want to add your own project (or know of one not listed from a past .Astronomy), follow the README instructions on Hack List GitHub repo to submit it.

See what hacks were done each year:

  • .Astronomy 1 - September 22nd-24th 2008 - Cardiff University
  • .Astronomy 2 - November 30 – Dec 4, 2009 - Lorentz Centre, Leiden
  • .Astronomy 3 - April 4-8, 2011 - New College, Oxford
  • .Astronomy 4 - July 9-11, 2012 - Haus der Astronomie and the International Wissenschaft Forum, Heidelberg
  • .Astronomy 5 - September 16-18, 2013 - New England Research & Development Center, Cambridge, Massachusetts
  • .Astronomy 6 - December 8-10, 2014 - Adler Planetarium, Chicago, Illinois, USA
  • .Astronomy 7 - November 3-6, 2015 - Justice & Police Museum, Sydney CBD, Australia
  • .Astronomy 8 - 20th – 23rd June 2016 - Pembroke College, Oxford, UK
  • .Astronomy 9 - 14 – 17th November 2017 - South African Astronomical Observatory, Cape Town, South Africa
  • .Astronomy 10 - 24 – 27th September 2018 - Space Telescope Science Institute, Baltimore, Maryland, USA
  • .Astronomy 11 - 22 – 25th October 2019 - Dunlap Institute, Toronto, Ontario, USA
